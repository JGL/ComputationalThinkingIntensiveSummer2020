1. The aim of these workshops.

  • Give you as much practical experience (aka failure) to allow you to level up quickly.
  • Having an organised way of recording what you are doing and your progress to your goal is just as important as choosing what your goal is.
  • I started recording everything in text files after I had a disagreement on a project and couldn't prove what I said happened, happened. Also, naming conventions.
  • Pair programming is encouraged via which ever platform you like (Skype, WhatsApp, Facetime, Zoom, TikTok, Instagram, whatever)
  • I have a bunch of exercises ready, if you get to the end of the content in each workshop - just message me!
