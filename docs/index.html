An intensive of lectures and workshops on Computational Thinking by Joel Gethin Lewis.

See https://github.com/JGL/ComputationalThinkingIntensiveSummer2020 for more information on the course.

Lectures:

  • Lecture 0: Introduction.
  • Lecture 1: Counting.
  • Lecture 2: Drawing.
  • Lecture 3: Mixing and Sculpting.
  • Lecture 4: Compressing.
  • Lecture 5: Linking.
  • Lecture 6: Looping.
  • Lecture 7: Consuming.
  • Lecture 8: Thinking.
  • Lecture 9: Doing.

Workshops: (this will change through the week, depending on how we feel and progress)

  • Workshop 0: Introductions.
  • Workshop 1: p5.js editor, errors, console & comments.
  • Workshop 2: shapes & drawing.
  • Workshop 3: colour.
  • Workshop 4: mouseX & mouseY and variables.
  • Workshop 5: objects and the map() function.
  • Workshop 6: random() and createGraphics() functions.
  • Workshop 7: conditional Statements.
  • Workshop 8: while and for loops.
  • Workshop 9: functions, classes and arrays.

Twice daily discussions will be based around whatever the group wants to talk about.
